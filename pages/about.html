Some of the content in the Collegeville Project comes from a capstone course on computer science methodologies taught by Michael Heroux since 1998. This material draws upon and develops best practices in oral and written discourse, with the basic goal of always improving each scientist's personal skills for effective communication.

Authors and Contributors
This site is developed and maintained by Michael Heroux @maherou with assistance from Duong Do @duongdo27

Its content is used as part of a Computer Science capstone course in research methodologies at the College of St. Benedict and St. John's University in rural Collegeville, Minnesota.
